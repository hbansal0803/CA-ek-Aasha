About Us

CA Ek Aasha is a mentorship initiative by CA Harshit & CA Hemant to guide aspiring Chartered Accountants through personalized study plans, test series, and emotional support. We believe every student deserves a mentor who understands their journey.

Mentorship Program

  • Personalized study targets and timetables
  • Chapter-wise test series and MCQ prep
  • Focus on high-weightage topics and exam strategy
  • One-on-one mentorship and doubt resolution
